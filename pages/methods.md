# Forecasting Methods

## Time Series Methods

- **Moving Averages** – smooth data by averaging over a window
- **Exponential Smoothing** – give more weight to recent observations
- **ARIMA** – autoregressive integrated moving average models

## Machine Learning Methods

- **Random Forest** – ensemble of decision trees
- **Neural Networks (LSTM)** – deep learning for sequential data
- **XGBoost** – gradient boosting for tabular data

## Qualitative Methods

- **Delphi Method** – structured expert consultation
- **Market Research** – surveys and focus groups
- **Scenario Planning** – explore multiple possible futures
